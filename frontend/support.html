Uptime, incidents & “is it down?”

Public status page: we do not operate a dedicated status dashboard (e.g. status.uploadm8.com) today. For widespread outages, check announcements in our Discord, posts on the Blog, and your email — we use those channels when we have confirmed, actionable information.

Account-specific issues: open Queue and the job detail view for error text; many failures are platform rate limits or OAuth re-auth. For a full ticket with optional screenshot, use Report a bug (signed-in). If something still looks wrong, email support@uploadm8.com with your account email and upload/job IDs.

Security incidents (breach, credential leak, vulnerability): security@uploadm8.com — see Security → Incident response.

Frequently Asked Questions — AI & Audio

What is audio transcription and why is it on by default?
Audio transcription (Stage 5.5) uses OpenAI Whisper to extract speech from your video before generating AI captions. By giving GPT-4o both the visual frames and what was said, captions are dramatically more accurate — especially for commentary, tutorials, or narrated content. It is enabled by default for all plans and can be turned off in Settings → AI Features.
Is my audio stored or used to train AI models?
No. The temporary WAV file extracted from your video is sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription and then deleted along with the rest of the job's temp directory. OpenAI does not retain API inputs for model training under their zero-data-retention API agreement. See our Privacy Policy §1 for full details.
Why did the audio stage skip on my upload?
The stage skips gracefully in several cases: no audio stream in the video, clip shorter than 3 seconds, clip longer than 10 minutes (cost guard), or if you have disabled it in Settings. Check the worker logs for Audio context skipped: to see the exact reason.
Does audio transcription use my AIC tokens?
Whisper is billed at the platform infrastructure level (~$0.006/min) and is not deducted from your AIC wallet. Your AIC tokens are used by GPT-4o for caption and thumbnail generation. The transcript improves caption quality but is an infrastructure cost covered by your plan, not charged to your AIC balance.

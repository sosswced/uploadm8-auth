## Ten hooks (rotate by audience)

1. **You create. We do the rest.** — Mass upload, accurate AI captions/thumbnails, consistent timing—manual posting off your plate.
2. **Mass content in. Consistent posts out.** — Batch the backlog; we package and schedule across four platforms.
3. **Struggle with consistency?** — We remove upload grind and timing stress so you can stay in create mode.
4. **Calendars schedule posts. UploadM8 packages the video.** — Soft compare vs Buffer/Later/Hootsuite-style tools.
3. **Credits that behave honestly** — Publishing credits for goes-live; AI credits for captions, hashtags, thumbnails. Balances move only when work runs.
4. **Batch Sunday, ship all week** — Deferred processing so scheduled posts do not go live early.
5. **Thumbnail Studio isn’t a side quest** — Prompt, recreate, and score—then publish from the same workspace.
6. **Agency Friday in one click** — Client/brand groups, flex credits, dedicated lane when the queue is loud.
7. **Your lap on screen. Your clip on four feeds.** — Drive analysis + telemetry for automotive creators (Pro+).
8. **Queue history you can screenshot** — Client accountability without a spreadsheet.
9. **Start free. No card. See the full loop.** — Starter tier conversion.
10. **Priority lanes when volume spikes** — Pro/Studio/Agency skip the free-tier line at peak.

## Positioning paragraph (soft compare)

Typical schedulers keep the calendar full; UploadM8 handles the heavy video work behind it—per-platform transcodes, AI captions and hashtags, thumbnail picks, smart scheduling windows, and credit balances you can read at a glance. Built for creators, studios, agencies, and automotive operators who need more than “pick a time and pray.”

How UploadM8 works

Last updated April 12, 2026. Built for creators and teams who need repeatable distribution without drowning in manual uploads — one presigned upload, resumable jobs, and a clear queue so publishing stays consistent while you focus on content. Workflow

  1. Connect — Link TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook in one place.
  2. Upload — Drop videos; direct-to-cloud presigned upload; set title, captions, and hashtags once.
  3. Schedule — Use smart windows or pick an exact publish time.
  4. Process — Transcode, optional Whisper transcript, thumbnails, and AI captions.
  5. Queue — Track status, cancel pending jobs, see platform results.
  6. Notify — Discord webhooks on success or failure.

Smart scheduling

  • Inputs: preferred windows, spacing, and volume.
  • Output: a plan that avoids collisions and keeps you consistent.
  • Overrides: manual time always wins.
Reality check: consistency beats chasing “perfect” post times. UploadM8 makes the operational part painless.

Audio & captions

After transcode, optional Whisper transcription feeds the AI caption step so hooks and hashtags reflect what was said, not only what was on screen. Silent or short clips skip gracefully. Tune this in Settings → AI.

1

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various functions — from keeping you logged in to remembering your preferences. We also use similar technologies including localStorage and session storage for application state management.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain on your device for a set duration). They can be set by us (first-party) or by third-party services we use.

2

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary
Always Active

These cookies are essential for the platform to function. They enable core features like authentication, security, and session management. You cannot opt out of these cookies — without them, the service cannot operate.

Purpose: User authentication, CSRF protection, session state
Duration: Session / up to 30 days for "remember me"

Preferences

These cookies remember your preferences to personalize your experience — such as your chosen theme (dark/light mode), dashboard layout, and notification settings. Disabling these means your preferences reset on each visit.

Purpose: Theme preference, UI layout, language settings
Duration: Up to 1 year
Party: First-party (UploadM8 only)
Analytics
Opt-Out Available

These cookies help us understand how users interact with UploadM8 so we can improve the platform. Data is aggregated and anonymized — we cannot identify individual users from analytics data. No behavioral advertising is conducted.

Purpose: Page views, feature usage, error tracking
Duration: Up to 13 months
Party: First-party and limited third-party analytics

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

In-App Controls

Visit Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences to opt out of analytics cookies. Strictly necessary and preference cookies cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

Browser Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may break UploadM8 authentication and core functionality.

International Data Transfers

Some of our subprocessors are located outside your country. When processing personal data from the European Economic Area, UK, or Switzerland, we rely on appropriate legal transfer mechanisms including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions where applicable.

All subprocessors contractually commit to maintaining data protection standards equivalent to those we apply to data processed within the US.

To request our Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with any subprocessor, contact privacy@uploadm8.com.

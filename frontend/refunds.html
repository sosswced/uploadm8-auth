2

AIC Token Purchases

AIC tokens are non-refundable and non-transferable. Once purchased, tokens are credited to your account immediately. Unused tokens do not carry over to new subscription periods unless explicitly stated in your plan.

Exceptions may be granted at our sole discretion in cases where tokens were consumed due to a verified platform error or system malfunction on our end. Contact billing@uploadm8.com within 7 days of the incident with your job IDs and a description of the issue.

3

Service Failure Credits

If UploadM8 experiences a service failure that materially impacts your ability to use the platform (not caused by third-party platform outages), you may be eligible for account credit:

  • Outage of 24+ continuous hours: Eligible for prorated credit for affected days
  • Repeated publishing failures due to UploadM8 bugs (not platform API issues): Case-by-case credit review
  • Data loss caused by our infrastructure: Full credit for affected billing period

Credits are applied to your account balance and automatically applied to your next billing cycle. Credits are non-transferable and not redeemable for cash. To request a credit, email billing@uploadm8.com with your account email, affected dates, and description of the issue.

Cancellation Process

Self-Service Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Billing → Open Billing Portal (then cancel in the portal). When you cancel, no future charges are made. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain access until then. You can reactivate later by resubscribing.

What Happens After Cancellation

  • No further charges; subscription ends at period end
  • You retain access until the end of your current billing period
  • At period end, your plan downgrades to free (or if you requested account deletion, your account is fully deleted)

Account Deletion vs. Cancellation

Cancelling your subscription stops future billing; you keep access until period end. To permanently delete all your data, use Settings → Security → Delete your account. Free users: Deletion is immediate and irreversible. Paid users: Deletion is scheduled for the end of your billing period; you retain access until then. There is no refund for any remaining subscription time upon account deletion.

5

Chargebacks & Disputes

Filing a chargeback without first contacting us will result in immediate account suspension during the investigation period. We will provide full transaction records to your card issuer and have a strong track record of winning legitimate disputes.

If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact billing@uploadm8.com first. Billing errors are resolved typically within 5 business days — far faster than a chargeback process. We are committed to resolving legitimate billing issues promptly and fairly.

Accounts involved in fraudulent chargebacks may be permanently banned and reported to payment fraud prevention networks.

6

Price Changes

We will provide at least 30 days written notice via email before implementing any price increases on your current plan. You may cancel before the new price takes effect to avoid the increase.

Annual plan pricing is locked for the duration of the subscription year at the rate charged at the time of purchase. Price changes take effect at annual renewal.

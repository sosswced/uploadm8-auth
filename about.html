About UploadM8

Last updated: January 26, 2026

UploadM8 is the operating system for multi-platform short-form distribution. One upload. One queue. Multiple destinations—TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels.

Mission: eliminate manual posting overhead so creators and teams ship content at enterprise cadence without losing creative control.

What we do

  • Single ingestion: upload once, distribute everywhere.
  • Centralized OAuth: connect platform accounts from one dashboard.
  • Queue + scheduling: stage posts, set target publish times, and track the countdown.
  • Operational visibility: success rates, failure reasons, and auditability.
  • Notifications: Discord webhooks for real-time outcomes.
  • Telemetry HUD: for driving content, overlay speed/HUD visuals (optional).

Core values

Reliability

Uploads and auth flows must be deterministic and observable—no mystery failures.

Creator control

You own the content, timing, captions, and privacy. The system executes.

Speed to publish

Reduce friction from idea → post; scale cadence without scaling headcount.

Roadmap direction

  • Commercial-grade KPI dashboard + export.
  • Smart scheduling inputs (time windows, spacing rules, platform caps).
  • Stripe billing + self-serve subscription management.
  • Team accounts, roles, audit trails.

What are PUT tokens and AI Credits?
⚡ PUT (Processing/Upload Tokens)
Consumed each time you process & post a video. Base = 10 PUT, +2 per extra platform, +5 for HUD burn, +5 for priority lane, +1 per extra thumbnail.
Example: 3 platforms, no HUD → 10 + 4 = 14 PUT
🤖 AIC (AI Credits)
Consumed by AI caption/hashtag/title generation. Cost depends on your tier's AI depth and frame count analyzed.
Example: Creator Pro (Advanced AI, 8 frames) → 4 + 1 = 5 AIC
Credits refill at the start of each billing period. Top-up packs never expire and stack on top of monthly allowance.

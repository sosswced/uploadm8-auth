UploadM8 Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how UploadM8 ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our multi-platform video publishing service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency.
1

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an UploadM8 account, we collect your name, email address, password (stored as a cryptographic hash), and billing information (processed by Stripe — we never store raw card data).

Content You Upload

Videos, thumbnails, titles, captions, hashtags, and other metadata you submit through our platform. This content is temporarily stored in Cloudflare R2 storage during processing and publishing. Original uploads are retained for 30 days after publication unless you delete them sooner.

Platform Integration Data

When you connect social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook), we receive and store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens necessary to publish on your behalf. We also receive basic profile information (username, channel/page name, profile photo URL) to display in your dashboard.

Usage & Technical Data

Log data (IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, timestamps), device information, and platform-specific analytics (views, engagement metrics) when available through platform APIs.

AI Feature Data

When you use AIC token-powered features, your video frames, transcripts, and metadata may be processed by third-party AI providers (Anthropic Claude API) solely to generate captions, titles, and thumbnails. This content is not retained by the AI provider beyond the processing request.

2

Platform User Data & OAuth Tokens

Important: UploadM8 acts as your agent when posting to social platforms. We only access the permissions you explicitly grant during OAuth authorization and never exceed those permissions.

Each platform integration collects the following data categories:

Platform Data Collected Purpose Retention
TikTok OAuth token, user ID, display name, avatar URL Video upload via Content Posting API v2 Until disconnected + 24 hrs
YouTube OAuth token, channel ID, channel name, thumbnail Shorts upload via YouTube Data API v3 Until disconnected + 24 hrs
Instagram OAuth token, business account ID, username Reels upload via Meta Graph API v21.0 Until disconnected + 24 hrs
Facebook OAuth token, page ID, page name, page category Reels upload via Meta Graph API v21.0 Until disconnected + 24 hrs

OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and transmitted exclusively over TLS 1.3. Tokens are stored in isolated database rows with access restricted to the publishing service only.

3

How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Processing and publishing your videos to connected platforms, generating AI-enhanced metadata, delivering scheduling and queue management.
  • Account Management: Authentication, subscription billing, plan enforcement, usage quota tracking.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Providing your Trill telemetry dashboard with cross-platform performance data pulled from platform APIs.
  • Platform Communications: Transactional emails (upload confirmations, failed job alerts, billing receipts, security notifications).
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Detecting unauthorized access, enforcing Terms of Service, maintaining audit logs.
  • Product Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage analytics to improve features. We do not sell individual behavioral data.
We do not sell your personal data, platform credentials, or content to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising targeting or behavioral profiling.

5

Data Deletion

You have three pathways to request deletion of your personal data:

Method 1: Self-Service Account Deletion

Navigate to Settings → Security → Delete your account (Danger Zone). This triggers immediate anonymization of your profile data, disconnection of all platform accounts, and queues your video files and database records for purge within 30 days.

Method 2: Platform Disconnect

Navigate to Settings → Platforms → Disconnect. OAuth tokens for that platform are revoked and deleted within 24 hours. This does not delete your UploadM8 account.

Method 3: Direct Request

Email [email protected] with subject line "Data Deletion Request." We acknowledge within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 days, providing written confirmation upon completion.

UploadM8 also maintains a dedicated data deletion page at uploadm8.com/data-deletion to comply with TikTok, Meta, and YouTube platform review requirements.
6

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your data only with the following categories of service providers (subprocessors) strictly necessary to deliver our service:

  • Infrastructure: Render (hosting), PostgreSQL (database), Redis (job queue), Cloudflare R2 (file storage)
  • Payments: Stripe (billing — PCI DSS Level 1 compliant)
  • AI Processing: Anthropic (Claude API — for AIC token AI features, no data retained)
  • Platform APIs: TikTok, YouTube/Google, Meta (for publishing only)

All subprocessors are bound by data processing agreements. See our full Subprocessors List for complete details including data locations and privacy policy links.

We may disclose your data if legally required by court order, government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety. We will notify you of any such disclosure to the extent permitted by law.

8

Children's Privacy

UploadM8 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are between 13 and 17, you may use UploadM8 only with verified parental or guardian consent.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe we have collected data from a minor without appropriate consent, contact us at [email protected] immediately.

9

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures including:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for all data at rest (tokens, sensitive fields)
  • Bcrypt password hashing with appropriate cost factor
  • Role-based access control with principle of least privilege
  • Automated 30-day database backup rotation
  • Comprehensive audit logging of all data access and modifications
  • Security incident response with 72-hour breach notification target

For complete details, see our Security Overview.

10

International Data Transfers

UploadM8 is based in the United States (Missouri). If you access our service from the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or other regions with data protection laws, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other approved transfer mechanisms where required. All international transfers are protected by the same security standards applied to U.S.-based data.

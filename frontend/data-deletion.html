Data Deletion

Last updated: April 5, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your UploadM8 account data and platform connection data.

What can be deleted

  • Account profile data (name, email).
  • Stored OAuth tokens for connected platforms.
  • Upload records and operational logs associated with your account (subject to retention rules).

How to request deletion

Self-service (fastest): In the app, open Settings → Security → Delete your account (Danger Zone). That disconnects platforms, removes access tokens on our side, and queues media and database rows for removal per our retention policy.

  1. Or email privacy@uploadm8.com with subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include the email address tied to your UploadM8 account.
  3. We may request verification to prevent unauthorized deletion.
Expected timeline: deletion requests are typically completed within 30 days, unless legal retention requirements apply.

Platform data

Disconnecting a platform in the dashboard stops future posting and removes access tokens stored by UploadM8. You can also revoke access directly in the platform’s security settings.

Contact

For deletion, privacy, or compliance questions: privacy@uploadm8.com · See Support — contact by department for other topics.

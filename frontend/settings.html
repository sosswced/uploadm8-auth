What are PUT tokens and AI Credits?
PUT (Processing/Upload Tokens)
Consumed each time you process & post a video. Base = 10 PUT, +2 per extra publish target (each platform when no groups, or each selected account when using groups), +5 for HUD burn, +5 for priority lane, +1 per extra thumbnail.
Example: 3 publish targets, no HUD → 10 + 4 = 14 PUT
AIC (AI Credits)
Consumed by AI caption/hashtag/title generation. Cost depends on your tier's AI depth and frame count analyzed.
Example: Creator Pro (Advanced AI, 8 frames) → 4 + 1 = 5 AIC
Monthly credits are released daily throughout the month (UTC cadence). Unused PUT and AIC roll over and stack—your wallet balance is never reset. Top-up packs never expire.

Danger Zone

Disconnect a platform account

Revokes your OAuth token at the provider and removes it from UploadM8. Your videos already published are unaffected.

Delete your account

Permanently removes all your data: uploads, platform tokens, billing history, and settings. This cannot be undone. Free accounts: deletion is immediate. Paid accounts: deletion is scheduled for the end of your billing period; you retain access until then.
